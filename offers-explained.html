Offers Explained

1 min read

On the Silverwing platform, products can have two different types of offers:

A Fixed Price Offer:

The price of the product does not change for the offer duration.

A Price Curve Offer:

During a Price Curve offer the unit price decreases as the cumulative quantity of units sold increases.

If the unit price drops during an offer, all Buyers who previously made purchases at a higher price receive Price Curve Credits to reflect the difference.

Price Curve Example

1. You buy 1 unit of this product for £100.00.

2. A week later you buy a second unit, with a unit price of £90.00.

3. After your second purchase, you receive £10.00 back in Price Curve Credits.

It’s important to note three things:

  1. Price Curve Credits can be withdrawn as cash as soon as they are received

  2. Purchases on the same Price Curve are aggregated across all organisations, not just for one

  3. If you buy more than one unit, you only pay the lowest unit price for each unit in that order
