Further Price Curve Example

1 min read

In this example we will show how purchases are aggregated across multiple organisations.

Example

1. Organisation X buys 1 unit of this product for £100.00.

2. Two hours later, Organisation Y buys 2 units of the product, they pay £80.00 for each unit (£160 in total)

3. Organisation X then receives £20.00 in Price Curve Credits to as the price has dropped to £80.00, and they originally paid £100.00.
